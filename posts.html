To view older posts, go to the posts directory of this site's source and add the post id to the end.

Post IDs are formatted as YYYY-MM-DD--HH-MM

Hours are 24 hour and are in the UTC+11:00 timezone.

For example, to view the post made on the 9th of September 2022 at 10:29 UTC you would go to:

https://github.com/TheBlueRuby/TheBlueRuby.github.io/tree/main/posts/2022-09-09--21-29.md

This will show the formatted markdown of the post.

To view the raw markdown, replace the "blob" in the resulting link with "raw"
